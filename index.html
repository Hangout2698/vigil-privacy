Privacy Policy

Effective: 11 February 2026

Vigil Intelligence ("Vigil", "we", "us", "our") provides a personal health intelligence platform that integrates biometric data from wearable devices with calendar and lifestyle context to deliver actionable health guidance. We take the protection of your personal and health data seriously.

This policy explains what data we collect, how we process it, and your rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and other applicable data protection laws.

01 Data We Collect

When you use Vigil, we collect and process the following categories of data:

Biometric & Health Data (via third-party APIs)

  • WHOOP: recovery scores, heart rate variability (HRV), resting heart rate, sleep stages, strain, SpO2, skin temperature, workout data
  • Garmin Connect: daily activity summaries, heart rate, HRV, sleep data, stress levels, body composition, training status, VO2 max estimates

Calendar & Schedule Data

  • Google Calendar: event titles, start/end times, duration, and location (read-only access)

Health Profile Data (provided by you)

  • Genetic predispositions, blood work results, injury history, training programs, health goals, and supplement protocols that you choose to provide

Account Data

  • Email address and authentication tokens required to connect your third-party accounts

02 How We Use Your Data

Your data is used exclusively to:

  • Generate personalised health, training, recovery, and nutrition guidance
  • Identify trends in your biometric data (sleep debt, HRV trends, training load)
  • Correlate your health metrics with life context (schedule, travel, stress) to improve recommendation quality
  • Display your health data back to you in summarised form

We do not use your data for advertising, sell your data to third parties, or use it to train machine learning models.

03 AI Processing

Vigil sends a structured summary of your recent health data and calendar context to the Anthropic API (Claude) to generate personalised advice. This means:

  • A subset of your health and schedule data is transmitted to Anthropic's servers for each advisory request
  • Anthropic's API does not retain input data beyond processing the request, in accordance with their data usage policy
  • No raw biometric data is stored by Anthropic — only the structured context summary is transmitted
  • You can review exactly what data is sent before each request

04 Data Storage & Security

  • Health data is stored locally on your device in an encrypted SQLite database by default
  • If you use our hosted service, data is stored on encrypted servers within the European Economic Area (EEA)
  • Authentication tokens are encrypted at rest
  • All API communications use TLS 1.2 or higher
  • We implement access controls to ensure only you can access your data

05 Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR)

We process your data under the following legal bases:

  • Consent (Article 6(1)(a) & Article 9(2)(a)): You explicitly consent to the processing of your health data when you connect your WHOOP, Garmin, and Google Calendar accounts. Health data is classified as special category data under GDPR, and we process it only with your explicit consent.
  • Contract performance (Article 6(1)(b)): Processing is necessary to provide you with the Vigil service you have subscribed to.

You may withdraw consent at any time by disconnecting your accounts or deleting your data through the application.

06 Third-Party Services

Vigil integrates with the following third-party services, each governed by their own privacy policies:

  • WHOOP — Privacy Policy
  • Garmin — Privacy Policy
  • Google — Privacy Policy
  • Anthropic — Privacy Policy

07 Your Rights

Under GDPR, you have the right to:

  • Access your personal data and receive a copy of it
  • Rectify inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Erase your data ("right to be forgotten")
  • Restrict processing in certain circumstances
  • Data portability — receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format
  • Object to processing based on legitimate interests
  • Withdraw consent at any time without affecting the lawfulness of prior processing

To exercise any of these rights, contact us using the details below. We will respond within 30 days.

08 Data Retention

We retain your health data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account or request data erasure:

  • All personal and health data is permanently deleted within 30 days
  • Authentication tokens are revoked immediately
  • Anonymised, aggregated data (which cannot identify you) may be retained for service improvement

09 International Transfers

Your data may be transferred to Anthropic's servers in the United States for AI processing. This transfer is conducted under Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) as approved by the European Commission, ensuring an adequate level of data protection.

10 Children

Vigil is not intended for use by anyone under 18. We do not knowingly collect data from minors.
